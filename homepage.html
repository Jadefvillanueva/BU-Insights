The Official Candidates of MMBU 2023, Crafting a Green Legacy

The A2C2 tree planting activity of the MMBU Candidates commenced today, October 23, 2023, at Matanag, Legazpi City earlier this morning. It is an initiative in line with Albay's fight against climate change about making a positive change for the environment, one step at a time. Their dedication to this cause sets an example for us all, reminding us that even the smallest deed can shape the future we'll inherit. By planting trees today, they're helping create a healthier and more sustainable world for tomorrow. Indeed, the MMBU Candidates are more than just what meets the eye.
